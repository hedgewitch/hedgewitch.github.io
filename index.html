Who We Are

Whim Kitchen is a permanent pop-up working out of Little Beast Brewing Beer Garden on SE Division St.. We take pride in our commitment to creating and preparing our menu items using seasonal ingredients sourced from local purveyors. Neighborhood farmers markets help inspire and shape our menu, which leads to an ever-changing approach here at Whim Kitchen. We make most of our menu in-house, from dressings and pickles, to our smoked meats and the bread for our baked-to-order sandos.
